For gums

Healthy gums do not bleed . Not hurt .

Gingivitis - This is an inflammation of the gums due to improper brushing or good oral hygiene. Gingivitis may be caused by common disease . Gums bleed. Consult your dentist for effective treatment.

Periodontitis - gum recession . Periodontal pockets are formed . Gums hurt . It is necessary dental treatment.

Advanced periodontitis. Deep periodontal pockets. Patients feel pain in teeth that are loose. Possible malaise , loss of tooth / teeth and violation of masticatory function . Treatment is long ...

For teeth

Dental plaque - bacterial film that forms continuously around all teeth.

Tartar - mineralized dental plaque that causes inflammation of the gums - gingivitis .

Caries - demineralization of the tooth surface caused by acids produced by bacteria in the mouth.
